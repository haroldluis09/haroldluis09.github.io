About Serene

Find Your Serenity

Serene is a self-care app designed to help you relax, refocus, and recharge. Whether you're seeking a moment of calm, guided breathing, or peaceful reset, Serene offers tools to support your wellness.

Created with a gentle aesthetic and mindful experience in mind, Serene is your digital escape from daily stress. Our mission is to bring tranquility to your fingertips — one breath at a time.

Privacy & Security

Your data is personal. We treat it that way.

Serene uses Firebase for authentication and storage. Your login credentials (passwords) are never visible to us and are managed securely by Firebase. All user-specific data (like hydration logs, journal entries, and preferences) is tied to your account and stored per day so you can pick up where you left off.

We do not sell your data. Data in transit is encrypted, and access is protected so only you (when authenticated) can read or update your own content.
