Privacy & Security

Your data is personal, and we treat it that way. Serene uses Firebase for authentication and storage. Passwords are never visible to us; they are securely handled by Firebase. All user-specific content (like hydration logs, journal entries, and preferences) is tied to your account and stored per day so you can pick up where you left off.

Data in transit is encrypted. Only authenticated users can read or update their own content. We do not sell your data. If you have questions or want to request deletion, reach out below.
